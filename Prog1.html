Timeline of Martin Luther King Jr (MLK)
  • Born January 15, 1929
  • Graduated High school at the age of 15
  • Recieved B.A. Degree from Morehouse College at the age of 19
  • Later graduated from Boston University at the age of 26, year 1955
  • Became leader of the nonviolent boycott in December of 1955
  • Enduring abuse, violence and arrest, MLK jr and the other boycotters won after 382 days
  • Blacks were now equal and MLK jr became president of the Southern Christian Leadership Conference in 1957
  • From 1957 to 1688,he gave speeches about injustice and protest across the U.S
  • Within these same years, he gave "I Have a Dream" speech at Washington D.C
  • Won Man of the year award in Time Magazine in 1963
  • Won Nobel Peace Prize in 1964
  • Unforunately, on April 4, 1968, he was assassinated on the balcony of a motel room
